      Ozito PXC

      Ozito Industries was established in Australia in 1993, and is committed to delivering value for money products to all Australians. Ozito products are sold throughout Australia and New Zealand exclusively through Bunnings Warehouse.

      We constantly research the needs of the market in terms of features and price to deliver DIYers the best value for money products available. We are committed to ensuring that the quality of our products exceeds the customers expectation every time, and to ensure this objective we carry out constant product evaluation and on-line manufacturing inspections by our team of local and overseas engineers. We are genuinely committed to ensure that everyone who purchases an Ozito product has their expectations exceeded and gets their job done.
